Overview

Thalappakatti Chicken Biryani is a renowned South Indian dish that hails from the town of Dindigul, Tamil Nadu. Known for its distinctive flavor and rich heritage, this biryani is a beloved choice among food enthusiasts who crave authentic Indian cuisine. The name "Thalappakatti" translates to "turban," referencing the founder's unique headgear, which has become an iconic symbol of this culinary brand.
